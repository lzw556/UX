p.cl1:before{content:"This is generated content at beginning ";color:navy;}
p.cl1:after{content:" This is generated content at ending";color:navy;}

这个P元素,class属性值是'cl1',UA应该在其内容开始前插入生成内容--'This is generated content at beginning',在其内容结束后插入生成内容--'This is generated content at ending',且生成内容的文本颜色应该是navy

p.cl2:before{content:"This is generated content at beginning ";color:olive;}
p.cl2:after{content:" This is generated content at ending";color:olive;}
p.cl2:first-letter{font-size:200%;}

这个P元素,class属性值是'cl2',UA应该在其内容开始前插入生成内容--'This is generated content at beginning',在其内容结束后插入生成内容--'This is generated content at ending',且生成内容的文本颜色应该是olive.然后在其第一行文本中,第一个字符的字体大小应该是P元素的2倍

p.cl3:before{content:"This is generated content at beginning ";color:maroon;}
p.cl3:after{content:" This is generated content at ending";color:maroon;}
p.cl3:first-line{background-color:aqua;}

这个P元素,class属性值是'cl3',UA应该在其内容开始前插入生成内容--'This is generated content at beginning',在其内容结束后插入生成内容--'This is generated content at ending',且生成内容的文本颜色应该是maroon.其第一行文本的装饰应该是underline

注意:经过测试,上面的例子在firefox和IE中符合预期.在chrome下,:first-line selector中的样式比如background-color,text-decoration会影响通过:before selector插入的生成内容的颜色样式
